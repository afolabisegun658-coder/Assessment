Spaghetti Jollof

A delicious Nigerian-style pasta cooked in rich tomato sauce and spices.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Easy

Dish Preview

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato paste
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Vegetable oil
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot and cook the spaghetti until soft, then set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions.
  3. Add tomato paste and blended tomatoes, then cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add seasoning, salt, and pepper, and stir well.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti into the sauce and mix thoroughly.
  6. Allow it to cook for another 5 minutes and serve hot.

Tips

For extra flavor, you can add vegetables like carrots and green peas. You can also include chicken or shrimp for a richer taste.

Nutrition Facts (Optional)

This meal provides carbohydrates for energy and essential nutrients from tomatoes and vegetables.
